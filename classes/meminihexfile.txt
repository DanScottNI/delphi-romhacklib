TMemINIHexFile v1.0
==============

Written by Dan
Released: 11/04/2005

This ZIP contains a descendant of the TMemINIFile that adds a new method
ReadHexValue, that loads hexadecimal values into integers. I also think
I may have overloaded one or two of the original reading methods to
allow you to not specify a default value.

Included:
=========

TMemINIHexFile
